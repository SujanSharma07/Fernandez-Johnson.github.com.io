Our Legal Services

Comprehensive Legal Solutions

We specialize in a wide range of legal areas including Family Law, Immigration Law, Wills & Estate, Litigation, Conveyancing, Buying and Selling of Business, and Criminal Law including Intervention Orders. Our multilingual team speaks Punjabi, Hindi, Tamil, and Bahasa Melayu for your convenience.

Family Law

Our family law practice provides comprehensive legal support for divorce proceedings, child custody arrangements, property settlements, child support, and domestic violence interventions. We understand the emotional nature of family disputes and offer compassionate guidance through these challenging times.

Consult with us about Family Law

Immigration Law

Our immigration law services cover visa applications, appeals at the AAT, representation at the Federal Circuit Court, citizenship applications, sponsor applications, and deportation defense. Our multilingual team ensures clear communication throughout your immigration journey.

Consult with us about Immigration Law

Wills & Estate

We assist clients in preparing wills, establishing powers of attorney, creating advance care directives, and managing probate applications. Our estate planning services ensure your assets are protected and distributed according to your wishes.

Consult with us about Wills & Estate

Litigation

Our litigation practice represents clients in court proceedings for civil disputes, contract breaches, property disputes, and debt recovery. We develop strategic approaches to achieve favorable outcomes while minimizing stress and costs.

Consult with us about Litigation

Conveyancing

Our conveyancing services handle property transfers, contract reviews, title searches, settlement coordination, and guidance on property regulations. We ensure your property transactions proceed smoothly and all legal requirements are fulfilled.

Consult with us about Conveyancing

Business Services

We provide comprehensive legal support for business acquisitions and sales, contract drafting and review, commercial lease agreements, business structure advice, and dispute resolution. Our goal is to protect your business interests at every stage.

Consult with us about Business Services

Criminal Law

Our criminal law practice offers representation for various offenses, intervention order assistance, bail applications, sentencing submissions, and appeals. We work diligently to protect your rights throughout the criminal justice process.

Consult with us about Criminal Law
